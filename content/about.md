# About

A collection of synthesized choral arrangements with Synthesizer V Studio I and RVC.  
Songs are mostly traditional Christian hymns.  

---

All of my choral arrangements and other resources are available [here in Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1nESecS1zvlXZ4XFU2lk9q0EWXgwYFf1v).  
The voice model used for melody/lead of every choral arrangements is available [here in HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/1358Adrian/rvc_temp_zipped/resolve/main/akari_kizuna_vocaloid_v4_raw_test_3_e256_s2304.zip?download=true). Dataset used for that model is found [in a zipped file](https://archive.org/download/raw-vocaloid/V4_KIZUNA_AKARI.zip) [here in archive.org](https://archive.org/details/raw-vocaloid).  
Synthesizer V Studio I editor and voicebanks are available [here in synthv.me](https://synthv.me/)
