Holy Wisdom, King of glory.  
Praise to You, Lord, Christ the Savior,  
Holy Wisdom, Son of God, King of glory.

Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, alleluia.  
Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, allelu, alleluia.  
  
*Sample verse used here:*  
Now, now — it is the Lord who speaks —  
come back to me with all your heart,  
for I am all tenderness and compassion. *[Refrain]*
