### Representative Text

*1*  
O Lord above,  
In boundless love,  
Please teach my heart to give,  
Not counting cost,  
Nor fearing loss,  
But serving while I live.  
  
*2*  
When battle calls,  
Though weakness falls,  
Still help me stand and fight,  
Through wounds and pain,  
No thought of gain,  
But striving for the right.  
  
*3*  
Though labor’s load  
Be rough the road,  
Let me not seek for rest,  
Nor seek reward,  
But serve you, Lord,  
And know Your will is best.  
  
*4*  
So let me be,  
In heart and knee,  
Obedient to Your call,  
To love, to give,  
In You to live,  
My God, my all in all.
