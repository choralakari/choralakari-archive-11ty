### Hymn Information

Folder: *Main I, no. 8*  
Name of tune used: *Revive Us Again*  
Composer: *Husband, John Jenkins (1760–1825); composed in 1815*  
Lyricist: *Mackay, William Paton (1839–1885); written in 1863*  
Sources used for representative text:
- *from [hymnary.org](https://hymnary.org/hymn/VT2020/91), Voices Together #91 (for verses 1–3 and 5)*

### Representative Text

*1*  
We praise thee, O God, for the Son of thy love,  
for Jesus who died, and is now gone above.  
  
*Refrain:*  
Hallelujah! Thine the glory, hallelujah! Amen!  
Hallelujah! Thine the glory, revive us again.  
  
*2*  
We praise thee, O God, for thy Spirit of light  
who has shown us our Savior and scattered our night. *[Refrain]*  
  
*3*  
We praise thee, O God, for the joy thou hast giv’n  
to thy saints in communion, these foretastes of heav’n. *[Refrain]*  
  
*4*  
All glory and praise to the Lamb that was slain,  
who has borne all our sins and has cleansed ev'ry stain. *[Refrain]*  
  
*5*  
Revive us again, fill each heart with thy love.  
May each soul be rekindled with fire from above. *[Refrain]*
