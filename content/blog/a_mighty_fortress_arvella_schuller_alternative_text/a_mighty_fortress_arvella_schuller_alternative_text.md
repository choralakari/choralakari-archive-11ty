### Representative Text

*1*  
A mighty fortress is our God,  
our stronghold and protection;  
He is our hope, our friend, our guide,  
the giver of salvation.  
Our Lord is God alone,  
He made the world His own;  
He speaks, and winds obey,  
He rules till endless day;  
His truth abides forever.  
  
*2*  
Great and eternal is our God,  
the God of all creation;  
He made the sun to shine through cloud,  
He formed earth's deep foundation.  
Rejoice and praise His name,  
His goodness will remain;  
with strong and guiding hand,  
firm shall His justice stand;  
His promise is eternal.
